Digital Literacy Tutorials

Simple step-by-step guides to help you use technology confidently.

1

Using Your Smartphone

  • Press the power button to turn on your phone
  • Swipe up or enter PIN to unlock
  • Tap icons to open apps
  • Use the back button (◁) to go back
  • Pinch to zoom in photos and maps
2

WhatsApp Basics

  • Open WhatsApp from your home screen
  • Tap the green chat icon to message someone
  • Type your message and tap the send arrow
  • Hold the microphone icon to send voice messages
  • Tap the video camera for video calls
3

UPI Payments (PhonePe, GPay, Paytm)

  • Open your UPI app (PhonePe, Google Pay, etc.)
  • Tap "Send Money" or "Pay"
  • Enter mobile number or scan QR code
  • Enter amount and your UPI PIN
  • Never share your UPI PIN with anyone!
4

Taking & Sharing Photos

  • Open the Camera app
  • Point at subject and tap the capture button
  • Open Gallery/Photos to view pictures
  • Tap Share icon to send via WhatsApp or email
  • Delete unwanted photos to save space
5

Using the Internet Safely

  • Open Chrome or your browser app
  • Type website address in the search bar
  • Look for 🔒 lock icon for secure sites
  • Don't click suspicious links in messages
  • Close tabs you don't need to save data
6

Email Basics

  • Open Gmail or your email app
  • Tap Compose (+) to write new email
  • Enter recipient email, subject, and message
  • Tap Send when ready
  • Check Inbox for replies from others
