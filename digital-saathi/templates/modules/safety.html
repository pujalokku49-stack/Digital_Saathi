Online Safety Tips

Protect yourself from scams and stay safe on the internet.

Beware of OTP Fraud

Never share OTP with anyone – not even bank employees or police. Banks never ask for OTP over phone.

Strong Passwords

Use at least 8 characters with letters, numbers, and symbols. Don't use birth dates or phone numbers.

Safe UPI Usage

Verify recipient name before paying. Never enter UPI PIN to receive money – you only need PIN to send.

Phishing Messages

Ignore messages claiming you won a lottery or need to update KYC via link. These are scams.

Social Media Privacy

Don't share personal details publicly. Set profiles to private. Be careful with friend requests from strangers.

Safe App Downloads

Only download apps from Play Store or App Store. Avoid APK files from unknown sources.

Public Wi-Fi

Avoid banking or UPI on public Wi-Fi. Use mobile data for sensitive transactions.

Fake Customer Care

Don't call numbers from Google search results. Use official numbers from your bank's website or card.

Report Cyber Crime

Report fraud at cybercrime.gov.in or call 1930 (National Cyber Crime Helpline).
