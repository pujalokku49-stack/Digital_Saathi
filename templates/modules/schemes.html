Government Schemes (India)

Important schemes for women, farmers, and senior citizens.

Farmers

PM-KISAN

₹6,000 per year in 3 installments for eligible farmer families.

  • Apply: pmkisan.gov.in or visit CSC center
  • Documents: Aadhaar, land records, bank account
  • Helpline: 155261 / 1800-115-526
Health

Ayushman Bharat (PM-JAY)

Free health insurance up to ₹5 lakh per family per year.

  • Apply: pmjay.gov.in or Ayushman Mitra at hospital
  • Eligibility: Based on SECC 2011 data
  • Helpline: 14555
Women

PM Matru Vandana Yojana

₹5,000 maternity benefit for first living child.

  • Apply: Through Anganwadi or ASHA worker
  • Documents: Aadhaar, bank passbook, MCP card
  • Helpline: Contact local Anganwadi center
Seniors

Indira Gandhi National Old Age Pension

Monthly pension for BPL citizens aged 60+ (amount varies by state).

  • Apply: Through District Social Welfare Office
  • Documents: Age proof, BPL card, bank account
  • Helpline: State social welfare department
Gas

PM Ujjwala Yojana

Free LPG connection for women from BPL households.

  • Apply: Through LPG distributor or online
  • Documents: Aadhaar, BPL ration card
  • Helpline: 1906
Digital

DigiLocker

Store government documents digitally – Aadhaar, certificates, licenses.

  • Apply: digilocker.gov.in or mobile app
  • Login: Use Aadhaar or mobile number
  • Helpline: support@digitallocker.gov.in
